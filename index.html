Firework shell

The firework shell is a miscellaneous item in the Fallout 4 add-on Contraptions Workshop.

Characteristics.
Locations.
Crafting.

Characteristics

Firework shells are launched from the firework mortar to either create a display or change the weather.

Locations

Must be crafted by the player character at a chemistry station or produced by a pyrotechnics mill.

Materials Requirements Produces
